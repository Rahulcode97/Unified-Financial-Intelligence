The problem

A week of finance work, buried in spreadsheets.

Three payment gateways. Hundreds of transactions a month. A finance team reconciling them by hand, line by line — 40+ hours every cycle, with errors that took two months to surface.

CCAvenuePayPalBank NEFT/RTGS
The system

I built one console that reconciles all three.

A Python engine that ingests every gateway, matches references against bank statements, flags GST mismatches, and produces a one-click monthly report. React frontend, Node and PHP APIs, MySQL on Google Cloud — designed and shipped solo.

Python · PandasNode.js · PHPReactMySQL
The result

From a week of work to under an hour.

₹63 Cr of transactions processed. 98.4% auto-match on reconciliation. ₹4.2 lakh in GST mismatches caught that manual review missed. The system pays for itself the first month it runs.
